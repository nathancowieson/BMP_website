Volunteering and getting involved

The hard work of maintaining and developing our Park is carried out by a diverse and friendly group of volunteers. If you'd like to get involved please come along to one of our Working Parties. These usually take place on the first Sunday of the month at 10 am. We meet up in the Performance Area of the park. Check our Facebook page for more up-to-date information. We usually work for two hours and stop for a tea break at 11.00. If you'd like to let us know that you're coming then we can look out for you but otherwise just turn up in old clothes. Take a mug, gardening gloves and any convenient gardening tools if you have them though there are usually some going spare if you don't. We'll see you there!

Its not ALWAYS sunny in the park for the working bee but we're always surprised how often it is!
