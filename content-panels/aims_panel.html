Promoting creativity and the Arts

We aim to make the park a space that supports and hosts engagement with creativity and the arts, particulary as this intersects with nature and the outdoors.

Safegaurding green spaces in the town

The amount of green space in and around Wantage has diminished dramatically in the last few decades. The park trust will safegaurd this vital resource for the benefit of us and for future generations.

A park for people AND wildlife

In managing and developing the park we aim to balance the needs of people and wildlife creating a safe home for native plants and animals and a place where people can enjoy them.

Partering with the wider community

The park does not exist in isolation either geographically, naturally or cultural and partnering with like-minded people and organisations can be an effective and enjoyable way to meet our other aims.
